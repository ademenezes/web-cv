About Me

Economist with expertise in development economics and econometrics, specialising in sector diagnostics, regulatory analysis, and investment modelling.

Currently serving as an Economist at the World Bank's Water Policy and Regulation unit (WKTWP), I lead economic analysis for country lending programmes and regional operations teams. My work spans regulatory gap assessments, econometric affordability analysis, and investment modelling, often delivered through interactive platforms that make complex analysis accessible to decision-makers.

With a PhD from LSE (economics concentration), I bring rigorous training in causal inference, spatial econometrics, and panel data methods. I have designed several analytical platforms including the WaterSAP sector diagnostic tool, the MACC investment simulator, and the Water Tariff Explorer, alongside three peer-reviewed publications.
